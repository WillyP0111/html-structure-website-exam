The first version of Microsoft Excel, initially released in 1985 for the Apple Macintosh, revolutionized spreadsheet software by introducing a graphical user interface and the concept of a "worksheet." It allowed users to organize and analyze data more intuitively than previous text-based spreadsheet programs, making it a pivotal tool in the world of business and data management. Excel has since evolved into a powerhouse application for data manipulation, analysis, and visualization, becoming an integral part of many industries worldwide.
